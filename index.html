What is Cenvora AI?

Cenvora AI is a SaaS platform that enables businesses to connect their WhatsApp Business and Instagram accounts and automate customer conversations using artificial intelligence.

The platform listens to incoming messages via official Meta APIs, processes them securely, and generates intelligent replies to help businesses support customers efficiently.
